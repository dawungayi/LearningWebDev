movieDB

Create an array of movie objects. Each movie should have a title, rating and hasWatched properties. The last piece is to iterate through the array and print out something that looks like:

  • You have watched "Die Hard" - 4.5 stars
  • You have not watched "Forrest Gump" - 5 stars
  • You have watched "Mr and Mrs Smith" 4 stars
  • You have not watched "The Smurfs" - 4.5 stars
